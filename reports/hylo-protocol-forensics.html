Leverage decay implicito

Il leverage di xSOL è variabile, non fisso. Se molta gente minta hyUSD, il leverage di xSOL aumenta. Se brucia hyUSD, scende. Difficile da prevedere senza monitorare il CR.

RISK 06

Concentration su SOL

Tutto il sistema dipende dal prezzo di SOL. xBTC arriverà in Q2 2026 per diversificare, ma per ora se Solana ha problemi, Hylo ha problemi.

03 — Stability mechanism

What happens when things go wrong

The obvious question: what if SOL crashes 50% in three days? Hylo has three automatic thresholds based on Collateral Ratio (CR), the ratio of total collateral value to hyUSD outstanding.

CR State Protocol action
> 150% Healthy Normal operation, minimal fees
130–150% Stability Mode 1 Dynamic fees discourage actions that worsen CR
< 130% Stability Mode 2 Stability pool activated: sHYUSD converts to xSOL
< 100% Critical xSOL → 0, hyUSD loses SOL volatility hedge

Key point: before the system collapses, there's an entire sHYUSD reserve acting as backstop. If SOL crashes hard, sHYUSD holders get part of their deposit converted to xSOL — accepting the risk in exchange for the amplified yield they've been earning.
